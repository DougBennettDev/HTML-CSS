Lesson Number One: Beginning Guitar

When you first decide you want to learn to play guitar, it's a good idea to figure out what music you like.
The type of music that you want to play will need the appropriate type of guitar to do the music justice
The raw edge of of punk rock won't sound great with a nylon string guitar,
in the same way a steel string guitar wouldn't be the ideal instrument for classical music
However, as you get better at playing guitar, you may want to explore all different types of sounds for different genres

Here's a table to help you decide which type of guitar is right for you

Genre Guitar Type
Jazz Hollow-body Electric
Rock Solid-Body
Classical Nylon String
Country Steel String

Once you have decided on what style of guitar you would like to play, its time to lear some chords!
